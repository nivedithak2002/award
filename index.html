About Us

The Women's Tabloid Awards stand as a highly esteemed occasion, a true celebration that pays
homage to the extraordinary triumphs of women hailing from diverse professional domains.
The paramount emphasis lies in spotlighting women leaders, chief executives, enterprising visionaries,
and enterprises skillfully guided by the female hand, all of whom are worthy recipients
of commendation for their exceptional and impactful endeavors spanning the globe.
